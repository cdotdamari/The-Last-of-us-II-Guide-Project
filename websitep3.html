Enemies

There are four types of enemies. The game provides the first set of enemies as the infected. The infected are a group of enemies that are "infected" with the cordyseps virus and they are dangerous enemies. They consist of Clickers, which are infected that are completely blind, but have great hearing and can one hit kill Abby or Ellie if not ready to defend. The next type are the runners, the runners are the base enemy and are the closest to human form. They run and attack fast. The next set of infected are the Stalkers, they hide in the darkness waiting for your guard to go down. Then there are the bloaters which are big tank like enemies along with shamblers which are very similar, but have less health. Finally leaving with the Rat King which is a huge amalgumation of Clickers into one big enemy. After them we have the 3 groups of human enemies WLF, Scars and Rattlers. Each group are very similar, but there are only some key differences. Scars are more primitive and use bows to fight, WLF's use military grade weaponry, and Rattlers use basically anything they can find. They are the "dirty fighters" of the game.
