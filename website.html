The Last of us II, is a game that puts you through the expierence of Ellie after the death of Joel Miller. Throughout the game you will need both Artifacts, and will need to fight difficult enemies. There is a total of 127 artifacts in the game, and about 30 hours of gameplay. The game is filled with lots of stealth objectives as well, these can range from hiding in tall grass, to hiding under a cart. The enemies have a view that if they see you they are alerted, and alert everyone around them to your exact location. To combat enemies and bosses both abby and Ellie are given collectable weapons throughout the game. There are a total of 12 weapons spread across playing as Abby and Ellie. Ellie is Equiped with a knife used for easy stealth take downs, and easy melee damage. she gets a total of 6 collectable weapons... They are

  1. Pistol
  2. Joel's Revolver
  3. Bow
  4. Rifle
  5. Shotgun
  6. Silenced SMG

Abby is the other playable character, she is the daughter of the doctor from the first game. Her role in The last of us II is to be the antagonist towards Ellie. The significance o her character comes from Joel Miller killing her father for Ellie. Which then Abby seeks out Joel in sight of revenge and succeeds in doing so. Starting the story of the game. Abby is more of a brawler. Abby has perks that help her fight with her bare fists, however, this comes at a price. Since she doesn't get a knife, she has harder stealth take downs and has to be strategic with how she fights certain enemies. Abby gets a total of 6 weapons as well, them being

  1. Military Pistol
  2. Hunting Pistol
  3. Cross Bow
  4. Assualt Rifle
  5. Double barrel Shotgun
  6. Flame Thrower

All weapons in The last of us II are upgradeable as well. Weapons use scraps at upgrade tables to upgrade the weapons damage, accuracy, recoil and ammo copacity. Along with these upgrades there are also supplement upgrades for characters skills. These skills can also help with weapon attributes and attacking attributes. Both Ellie and Abby have different upgrades for each of their weapons and supplements. Abby is more based around upgraiding guns and melee attacks, while Ellie is more based around stealth and craftables.
